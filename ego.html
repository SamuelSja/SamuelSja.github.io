The Grumpster

In this game the player has to smash eggs to collect yolks and shells. The player then needs to feed the shells to the grumpster to keep him happy. It was made in Rust using Tetra.
The Grumpster Download
To play the game, download and extract the zip file. Then run the egg_smasher.exe file.

Music Player

This is probably the most helpful program I have made. It allows me to play music downloaded on my computer in an organized fashion. The reason this was so helpful to me was because my school refused to let students use WIFI on personal computers, but with this, I could still listen to music. This was made with Rust and Tetra.
Music Player Download
To run the music player download and unzip the Music Player Download, then run the music_player.exe file.
Note that this contains music from Stardew Valley
